Prittyush Magan

The Time of Wabi-Sabi

4BCA

Wabi-sabi,
the beauty in imperfection,
the elegance in simplicity,
the wisdom in age.
The humble pottery bowl,
cracks and flaws revealing
its journey through time.
Yet, it remains cherished
for its unique character
and its capacity to hold
the nourishment of life.
Wabi-sabi,
a reminder to embrace
our own imperfections,
to find beauty in the ordinary,
and to honour the passing of time.
everything.
